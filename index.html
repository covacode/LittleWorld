Hello we're Little World

The story about us!

We provide support and comfort to families who want their children to be under the care of a loving and caring family. Our school provides a safe and learning enviromment where children will engage in fun and learning. Feeling safe and comfortable being children.

The rescue of values and principles is one of our care pillars; Where education, manners, discipline and academic formation unite to help shape the professionals of our future.

We are Spanish immersion since both of our founders are of Hispanic origin (Venezuela & Colombia) both are fluent Spanish speakers and they have English as a second language.

Our Curriculum

Our is meeting milestones, themed curriculum includes early literacy skills, beginning math concepts, music and movement, sensory play, gross motor development, social/life skills, art and dramatic play all while Immersed in the Spanish Language. Supporting their independence as promoted by Maria Montessori.

Your child will be exposed to

Literacy

  • Upper and lowercase letters
  • Letter sounds
  • Pre writing fine motor practice
  • Name recognition activities
  • Vocabulary growth
  • Exposure to weekly teacher read aloud books and access to age-appropriate theme.
  • Learn and sing songs and nursery rhymes

Math

  • Number and quantity recognition
  • 2 Dimensional shapes
  • Sorting objects based on common characteristics
  • Calendar

Social and Life Skills

  • Cleaning up skills and songs
  • Basic hygiene protocols
  • Daily manners lessons
  • Empathy and Appropriate social skills
  • Sharing
  • Effective verbal communication skills
  • Coping and calming down strategies
  • Exploration and Art
  • Sensory activities
  • Dramatic play opportunities
  • Weather knowledge
  • Independence skills

Our Programs

The ultimate goal of an education is independence and learn by being happy through play. At Little World we offer this to your child, in the fullest possible sense. Your child will gain the knowledge, confidence, creativity, and social ability that allow him to choose his own goals, whatever they may be, and pursue them over time. These are the qualities of character and mind that add up to a fulfilling adult life.

Teaching children through play with developmentally based focus on science and sensory, math, literacy, writing, art, and fine and large motor skills.

Our Staff

Gellelyn Montiel

Founder and Teacher

Our founder is of Venezuelan origin, who can fluently speak the Spanish language and has English as a second language. She has 12 years of experience in educational training and teaching children of different ages, has experience working with children with special needs (autism), and has had a great opportunity to work under the Montessori method.

Yeinis Valderrama

Founder and teacher

Experienced Child Care Provider with over 12 years experience in providing supervision and guidance to children within day care. Highly accomplished in creating educational and recreational activities designed to develop children’s psychological, emotional, behavioral, and social skills. As bilingual (English/Spanish) and Montessori method experienced teacher she has been dedicated to helping children better understand the world around them and developing relationships with parents to enrich children’s lives through play and learning.
